About COVIDify

COVIDify is a COVID-19 Dashboard.

Our main aim is to educate the common man who isn't "tech-savvy" per se, with regards to the evolving Covid situation in a fun, easy and interactive way. A user will be able to choose from following visuals - graphs, map and tables. He/She will also be able to telescope into local issues (near him/her) or zoom out to issues at a larger scale such as his state or country.
